Desert Safari Tour

Between 7 and 7.30 am, we'll pick you up from your hotel and hit the road towards the Dubai desert in a 4x4 land cruiser. Leaving the skyscrapers and unusual buildings of the city behind, we find ourselves surrounded by the natural beauty of the desert. After a 45 minute drive, we'll reach the starting point of the safari tour.

Having adjusted the pressure in our tyres, the land cruisers will tackle the dunes at top speed, so you can feel the adrenaline of each slope, losing ourselves in miles of sand in a 4x4, combining nature and adventure in the same experience.

Completing our safari in the Dubai desert we'll try our hand at sandboarding, similar to snowboarding, just on sand! You're sure to love it!

Then, we'll head to Al Khayma camp. We'll get off the jeep and enjoy an Arabica coffee accompanied by some typical sweets. Once we've regained our strength, we'll go for a camel ride. Contemplating the immensity of the desert from the back of this animal is really fascinating.

We will finish the activity between 11 am and 12 pm, leaving you at your hotel.

Looking for more adrenaline?

When making your reservation you can choose the intensity of your desert safari. If you're one for strong emotions, the extreme safari will guarantee you jumps, drifts, and manoeuvres by the driver which are sure to get the heart pumping!

Hotel pick up

This tour includes pick up service from hotels and apartments located in Dubai City and Port Rashid Cruise Port. Pick up from Jebel Ali, Al Maha Resort and Bab Al Shams is not included.

Evening safari

If you wish, you can do the Dubai desert safari tour in the evening. In this case, you'll also enjoy a barbecue dinner and a belly dance show.
